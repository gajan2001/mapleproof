Delete my account

Your right under PIPEDA. We'll execute your request within 30 days.

This is permanent Once you submit this request, your name, DOB, ID number hash, photos, and all associated records will be deleted from our database. You can re-register at any time, but your barcode token will change. Past audit log entries are retained for 5 years for compliance purposes (the entries reference only your token, not your personal information after deletion).

Option 1: Submit a deletion request

We'll review and execute within 30 days. You'll receive email confirmation when complete.

You can find this on your pass card under "ID · ".

Option 2: Immediate deletion

Delete your account right now. Your data will be removed within seconds. No review, no email confirmation.

Other privacy rights

Beyond deletion, under PIPEDA you also have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction — request fixes to inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent — same effect as deletion

Email privacy@mapleproof.example for any of these. We respond within 30 days as required.

If you're unsatisfied with our response, you can complain to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada at 1-800-282-1376.
