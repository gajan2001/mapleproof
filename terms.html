4. What Mapleproof does and doesn't do

What it does

  • Reads the AAMVA-format barcode on your provincial ID
  • Calculates your age tier (19+ / 25+) live, on every scan
  • Captures your face and verifies it via a liveness challenge
  • Compares your live face to the photo on your ID
  • Issues a barcode that retailers can scan to see this verification result

What it doesn't do

  • It does not authorize sales. A successful scan is information; the cashier still must check your ID.
  • It does not verify the authenticity of your physical ID document.
  • It does not guarantee acceptance at any retailer. Each retailer makes their own decision.
  • It does not replace any legal requirement under Ontario, federal, or municipal law.
